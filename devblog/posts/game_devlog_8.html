Game Devlog #8!


TL;DR: Still working on my digital painting skills. I tried to make a small game with my 2d assets and I got the feeling that I need to get better at digital painting to really create the 'feel' I want in my game.

Hi!
So I'm still learning digital painting which is really fun! I know that this is not the "main" thing I should be doing now because I want to be a game developer and not a digital artist. But I've decided that I want to get better at digital painting and I know this is the "longer way" to make a game, but this is what I want to do. Plus if I won't succeed in game development, atleast I got better at digital painting so I gained a skill!

So I've decided to document my process of improving my digital painting and I posted a speedpaint video of me painting a tomato. I want to be able to look back on my drawings and see the improvement over time. Here is the video:

So I know it's not the best tomato you've seen in your life, but it's something! I gotta say that I get a motivation boost everytime I succeed in a little thing, it keeps me going and I really hope that I would continue with this.
